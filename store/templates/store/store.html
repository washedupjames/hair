About me and my products


Welcome to tee’s nature oil, where we celebrate the beauty of natural hair through the power of nature.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to offer hair care solutions that nurture and enhance your hair's natural health and beauty without the harsh chemicals found in many conventional products. Whether you are struggling with hair loss or thinning, split ends, damaged hair.



Trust our products to help repair and grow your hair.

We believe that what you put on your hair should be as pure as the earth itself. Our products are crafted with this in mind, combining traditional wisdom with modern science. We focus on ingredients that are not only effective but also sustainable, ensuring that our footprint on the planet is as light as the touch of our products on your hair.




Hair oils are made from natural herbs, essential oils, and medicinal plants that nourish the scalp and promote hair health. Here are some key benefits:


Promotes Hair Growth
Ingredients like Bhringraj, Amla, and Brahmi stimulate hair follicles, encouraging thicker and longer hair growth.

Reduces Hair Fall
Strengthens hair roots with herbal extracts such as Neem, Hibiscus, and Ashwagandha, reducing breakage and hair loss.

Prevents Dandruff & Scalp Infections
Anti-inflammatory and antifungal properties of Neem, Tea Tree Oil, and Fenugreek help control dandruff, itchiness, and scalp infections.

Delays Premature Graying
Herbs like Amla and Henna help maintain natural hair color by preventing premature graying.

Nourishes & Strengthens Hair
Rich in essential fatty acids and vitamins from Coconut Oil, Sesame Oil, and Almond Oil, which deeply nourish and strengthen hair strands.

Improves Blood Circulation
Massaging the scalp with Ayurvedic oils enhances blood flow, ensuring better oxygen and nutrient supply to hair follicles.

Adds Shine & Softness
Natural ingredients like Aloe Vera, Hibiscus, and Castor Oil make hair soft, silky, and manageable.
